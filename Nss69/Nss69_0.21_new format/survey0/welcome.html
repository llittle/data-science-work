NSSO conducted an all-India survey of urban slums in the country in the 69th round of survey during July 2012 to December 2012. This survey is the fifth all-India survey of NSSO on the subject and was conducted in randomly selected sample urban blocks spread over the entire geographical area of the country. The previous NSS rounds during which survey on slums were conducted are 31st (July 1976 to June 1977), 49th (January to June 1993), 58th(July to December 2002) and the 65th (July 2008 to June 2009). This survey recorded some key characteristics of slums such as number of slums, approximate number of households living in the slums, proportion of slums without electricity, drainage facilities, a garbage disposal system along with their proportions with the majority of the households having pucca structure, tap water as major source of drinking water, and a pucca road within the slum, etc.
